Gladiator II

Description

Actors: Dulquer Salmaan Mrunal Thakur
director Hanu Raghavapudi
Producer C.Aswani Dutt
Story The film follows Lieutenant Ram (Dulquer Salmaan), an orphan in the Indian Army, who receives anonymous love letters from Sita Mahalakshmi (Mrunal Thakur). Ram sets out to find Sita and propose to her, but when he returns to his camp in Kashmir, his letter to Sita doesn't reach her.
