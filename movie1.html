Kushi

Description

Actors: Vijay Deverakonda Samantha Ruth Prabhu
director Shiva Nirvana
Producer Naveen Yerneni and Y. Ravi Shankar
story The movie is about Viplav (Vijay Deverakonda), a BSNL employee, and Aradhya (Samantha Ruth Prabhu), who pretends to be a Pakistani begum. The movie explores the complexities of relationships and the troubles that arise when couples have different religious upbringings.
