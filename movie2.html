Gladiator II

Description

Actors: Nithiin Nithya Menen Ajay and Sindhu Tolani
director S.S.Raju
Producer Vikram Kumar
Story A Telugu-language family drama film written and directed by Vikram Kumar. It stars Nithiin and Nithya Menen in the lead roles, with Ajay and Sindhu Tolani in supporting roles. The film was released on February 24, 2012 and was one of the highest-grossing films of the year. It won the Nandi Award for Best Home-viewing Feature Film.
