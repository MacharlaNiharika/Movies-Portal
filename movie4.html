Shyam Singa Roy

Description

Actors: Nani, Sai Pallavi, Krithi Shetty
director Rahul Sankrityan
Producer Venkat Boyanapalli
Story Shyam Singha Roy is a writer and social reformer who comes from a conservative family in West Bengal. He falls in love with Maitreyi, a devadasi who dances in a temple during Navaratri. Shyam rescues Maitreyi from the temple's high priest, who sexually abuses her, and becomes a crusader against social injustice.
